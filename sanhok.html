SANHOK

Sanhok is a 4x4 km island. By physically being
in the jungles and mountains in a humid asian
country, it felt the breeze, smelled the air and
heared the sounds. All of these experiences
are help the team create a unique map
experience.

The map is inspired by a wide variety of
island in thailand and the philippines.

Location: Thailand / Philippines

Theme: Jungle / Rain Forest

Terrain: Grass, Woodland, Urban

Platforms: PC, Xbox, PS4 & Mobile

Servers: Ranked

Game modes: All
