MIRAMAR

Miramar is a 8x8 km map with an open desert
plain and rural areas. Miramar offers unique
terrain and dense urban areas. With an island
to the south east home to prison and smaller
villages, the entire area is cut off from the west
and north-east by canyons and mountains.

Location: Mexico

Theme: Desert

Terrain: Dirt, Urban, Sea

Platforms: PC, Xbox, PS4 & Mobile

Servers: Ranked

Game modes: All
