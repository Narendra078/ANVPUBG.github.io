VIKENDI

Vikendi is a 6x6 km island on the Adriatica Sea.
Vikendi is enveloped in dense/thick forest and
another aspect of the island is its warm
Mediterranean costline and snowy mountains
in the central area of the map.

The map is inspired by Slovenia to create the
visual design of the map.

Location: Adriatic Sea

Theme: Snow Forest

Terrain: Snow, Mountainous, Sea

Platforms: PC, Xbox, PS4 & Mobile

Servers: Ranked

Game modes: All
