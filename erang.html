ERANGEL

Erangel is the base and best map of the game.
This map is of 8x8 km. Cities and the military
base have plenty of decent loot,but you won't
be the only one thinking the same thing.
A mix of urban areas and wide open farmland
create a wide variety of battles,ranging from
intense close range firefights in streets and
buildings, to long range sniping.

The name Erangel was chosen, using
PLAYERUNKNOWN'S daughter's name,
Eryn, as inspiration.

Location: Russia

Theme: Forest

Terrain: Grass, Woodland, Urban, Sea

Platforms: PC, Xbox, PS4 & Mobile

Servers: Ranked

Game modes: All
